CROSS-ROADS ENTERPRISE INTERNATIONAL



CREN Forum wants to be a leading company in Botswana dedicated to fostering the professional growth and development of young professionals across various sectors including international trade, investment, and property. By owning and managing specialized forums under its umbrella, Cren Forum aims to provide comprehensive support through education, networking, mentorship, and advocacy.


Key Forums Under Cren Forum

1. Botswana Youth Trade Network (BYTN)
2. Botswana Youth Investment Network (BYIN)
3. Botswana Youth Property Network (BYPN)
4. Botswana Youth Business Forum (BYBF)
